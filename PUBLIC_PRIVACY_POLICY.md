# Privacy Policy for Mobile Bidding Marketplace

**Last Updated: February 14, 2026**

## Introduction

Mobile Bidding Marketplace ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide

**Product Listings:**
- Product titles, descriptions, and categories
- Product photos (captured via camera or selected from gallery)
- Asking prices and bid amounts
- Listing timestamps

**Account Information (if implemented):**
- Email address
- Username
- Profile information

**Bidding Activity:**
- Bid amounts and timestamps
- Bid history
- Product interactions

### 2. Information Collected Automatically

**Device Information:**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version (iOS/Android)
- Browser type
- IP address
- App version

**Usage Data:**
- Pages viewed
- Features used
- Time spent in app
- Navigation patterns

### 3. Camera and Photo Access

We request camera and photo library access to:
- Take photos of products for listings
- Select existing photos from your device
- **Your photos are only used for product listings you create**
- We do NOT access your photo library without explicit permission
- Photos are only uploaded when you submit a product listing
